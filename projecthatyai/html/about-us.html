A Place of Comfort and Contemporary Living

Hatyai Apartments is a modern residential development established with a vision to redefine urban living in the heart of Hat Yai. Founded in 2015, the property was created to offer residents a harmonious blend of comfort, convenience, and contemporary design — an ideal retreat for those who appreciate a balanced lifestyle. Proudly standing as a symbol of quality living in southern Thailand, Hatyai Apartments provides well-designed accommodations complemented by essential amenities that enhance daily life. Within its calm and welcoming environment, residents enjoy a full-service fitness center overlooking the pool, featuring a range of modern equipment from cardio and resistance machines to free weights and dumbbells. The residence also features a serene swimming pool surrounded by lush greenery, creating a peaceful atmosphere perfect for relaxation and recreation after a long day.

More than just a place to live, Hatyai Apartments is a community where comfort meets simplicity. Every detail from thoughtful design to attentive management reflects our commitment to creating a space that feels like home. At Hatyai Apartments, we believe that true luxury lies in everyday comfort and care. Welcome to your new way of living — where modern living, convenience, and tranquility come together seamlessly.
